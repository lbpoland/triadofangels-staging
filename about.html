About Triad of Angels & ToA Studios

Faith. Fire. Innovation. All Genres. All Digital. All for You.

Our Story

Triad of Angels is a modern, faith-rooted creative brand built for a new era of music and digital storytelling. The Triad of Angels female group is a digital creation inspired by three real-life triplet sisters—reimagined as a single, unified creative force. Our mission is blending faith, technology, and every genre imaginable: worship and gospel, cinematic and orchestral, pop and country, rock and metal, EDM and trance—crafted with intention, heart, and relentless quality.

ToA Studios (Triad of Angels Studios) is the umbrella that powers everything beyond the music: publishing, games, apps, art, design systems, music videos, digital products, and the full creative ecosystem around our worlds and stories. We build with purpose and discipline—so every release feels cohesive, premium, and unmistakably Triad of Angels.

Our Creative Process

We believe music and storytelling are about connection—spirit, emotion, and meaning—no matter the tools. We use modern digital production and AI-assisted workflows as part of our pipeline, but the vision, direction, and final quality bar are ours.

We know not everyone feels the same way about AI in music. That’s okay. For us, this is a craft: concepts, structure, lyrics, revisions, mastering decisions, and release polish—shaped with care so the final result lands with impact.

We’re always learning, refining, and leveling up. If you have feedback—technical, creative, or listener experience—send it through our Contact page. Thank you for being here. Our mission is simple: share hope, strength, and light with anyone who wants to listen.

Our Creative Workflow & Tools

Triad of Angels & ToA Studios harness the most advanced tools and workflows in the music and creative industries.
Music creation: Suno (AI music generation), DAWs, Adobe Audition, eMastered for mastering, with a focus on pro arrangement: full song structures (intros, verses, choruses, bridges, instrumentals, breakdowns), genre-authentic sound design, and all lyrics written in-house for every song and album.
Visuals & Video: RunwayML for generative video and editing, Hedra AI for lip-syncing, Revid AI for smart editing, Blender, Unreal Engine, and Unity for 3D content and game development, DaVinci Resolve for pro video work and music videos, Adobe tools for art and content.
Ownership: While using cutting-edge AI, all lyrics, song layouts, melodies, and arrangements are original and copyright belongs to Triad of Angels & ToA Studios.

Genres we cover: Gospel, Worship, Christian, Cinematic, Pop, Rock, Metal, Heavy Metal, Ballads, Country, Jazz, Funk, Hip Hop, Rap, R&B, Dance, Trance, EDM, Drum & Bass, Dubstep, Uplifting Trance, Hard Trance, Psytrance, Techno, Alternative, Classical, and more.
Future: We’re expanding into interactive games (web, Android, iOS), digital art, ebooks, and all digital products.
